Recipe

Chocolate cake

"This choclate cake recipe is a classic. This version of the recipe is fast and quick and your kds will love it."


Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 mins 35 mins

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 4nos.

Instructions

1

 Melt the Chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven
